Dive into a realm where retro meets mystique with the Mystice Times font. 
Channeling the enchanting ambiance of 80s thriller movies, this serif typeface echoes tales of mystery wrapped in professionalism. 
Its characters, though rooted in the cinematic charm of yesteryears, are tinged with an unpredictable, slightly eccentric flair. Yet, there's an undeniable allure in its design - a suave nod to 70s funk, blending seamlessly with 80s charisma. 
Perfect for those seeking a touch of nostalgia, Mystice Times stands out as a unique blend of the eerie and elegant, lending any project a timeless, captivating vibe.

The font is built with advanced OpenType functionality and has a guaranteed top-notch quality, containing stylistic and contextual alternates, ligatures, and more features; all to give you full control and customizability. It has extensive lingual support, covering all Latin-based languages, from Northern Europe to South Africa, from America to South-East Asia. It contains all characters and symbols you'll ever need, including all punctuation and numbers.

An artisan of type, Mans Greback's work is a confluence of artistry and practicality. Through his fonts, he narrates stories, each typeface echoing a unique emotion, era, or culture.
